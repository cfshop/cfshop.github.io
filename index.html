FAQs

Who is CRYPTFLY?
CRYPTFLY is owned by two brothers who love horror and heavy metal. One is a gifted artist and the other an IT professional. We collaborated on this brand to create apparel that we wanted to wear.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! We currently offer international shipping through our partners at Printify, although it may not be available for specific items. Scroll down to the shipping methods under any item to see its specific shipping options.
Have an idea for a custom T-shirt?
We have lots of ideas that don't make it to the store. We would love to collaborate with you to create a custom design just for you. Contact us and we would love to create something special.
Do you ever offer in-person or pop up stores?
We are currently booking a few horror conventions! Stay up to date with our events section to see if we are in a crypt near you!
What is CORS?
CORS, aka Cross-Origin Resource Sharing, is a mechanism that enables many resources (e.g. images, stylesheets, scripts, fonts) on a web page to be requested from another domain outside the domain from which the resource originated.
